Simulation Analysis

Compare theoretical and simulated results.
The theoretical minimum was 140670, which is 70.34% of the 200,000 maximum bandwidth. The theoretical maximum was 170,850, which is 85.43% of the 200,000 maximum bandwidth. The results from the acceptance tests monitor gave the network utilization as 56.32%, or 112,640 for the actual simulated bandwidth. This was below our theoretical results by quite a large margin.
